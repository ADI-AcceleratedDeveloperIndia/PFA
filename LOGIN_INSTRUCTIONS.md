The PFA app **does not have a pre-created account**. You need to **register first** to create your account.

## ğŸ“ How to Register & Login

### Step 1: Go to Login Page

1. Open your PFA app URL (local: http://localhost:3000 or your Vercel URL)
2. You'll see the **Login** page

### Step 2: Register a New Account

1. On the login page, click the **"Register"** tab (or button)
2. Enter:
   - **Email**: Any email address (e.g., `test@example.com` or your real email)
   - **Password**: At least 8 characters (e.g., `password123`)
3. Click **"Register"**
4. You'll be automatically logged in

### Step 3: Login Later

After registering, you can login with:
- **Email**: The email you used to register
- **Password**: The password you set
